The Capital of Peru: Lima

We only spent a day in Lima as most people online recommended not spending too much time in the capital. As a result, this day was super chill. It was definitely needed after the previous days of hiking… we roamed around the city and simply ate a lot of good food by the water and visited a local market with food stalls.

We arrived back in Cusco by ~5 pm as we left Rainbow Mountain around 1 pm and stopped for lunch at the same place we had breakfast. We met up with the couple from our hike to Machu Picchu for dinner and a drink as we would be flying out that night. We went to a crepe restaurant that they recommended… really good food!
