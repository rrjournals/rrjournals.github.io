A Vancouver Food Tour

This was my first post-pandemic trip! Not sure if we are living in a pandemic world or not anymore but I considered this trip to have been “after the pandemic.” I was triple-vaxxed and had already had COVID, so I was looking forward to travelling for the first time since 2019, trusting my immune system was ready. This would be the first of three planned vacations this year! Funny enough, I had never been to Vancouver before this trip but went to Vancouver twice in the month of May 2022… we had a conference for work that took us to Calgary and Vancouver just two weeks before! However, I was only in Vancouver for 36 hours for the conference and hardly got to see the place, so I would not consider that a visit. This trip would be a chance to see the city and eat a lot of food...
