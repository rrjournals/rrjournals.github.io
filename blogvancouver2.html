Tacofino and Kits Beach

The next day started off similar! We went to Tacofino for lunch/breakfast – it’s a Mexican restaurant that plays off the name Tofino (a coastal town on Vancouver Island). The music was bumping. After that, we went to Kits Beach.

Mhmmm... Tacos.

Kits Beach was nice, but the weather was a bit cold even though it was sunny. We spent about an hour and then headed back. The view was still super nice, but it was windy and there was no chance I was going in the water. That night, we did a bit of a fancier dinner and dressed up. We were trying to go to Cactus Club, but it was super packed without a reservation. The original Indian restaurant we had was also booked. We ended up at this Italian restaurant.
