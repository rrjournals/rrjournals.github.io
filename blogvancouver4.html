Lynn Canyon and Sushi Dragon

This was the last day for a couple of my friends. They had an afternoon flight, so we got up early to check out. We dropped our bags off at one of our co-worker’s, who lives in Vancouver, places and then went to Lynn Canyon for a hike around 9:30 am. The hike was super nice! It was not a hard trail by any means, and we crossed a few little rivers. We did see a few dogs, which was super cute.

River on the hike!

Afterwards, we made sure to get sushi at Sushi Dragon. It was so reasonably priced and was quite good (note: I am no sushi connoisseur, but they do have a 4.3 rating online). After this, two of our friends headed to the airport.

Mhmm... sushi.

We then headed back to our new Airbnb for the last night and parted ways! Two of my friends had flights and I met up with an old friend from school for Korean Tacos nearby at a place called “TAKO” which takes the first two letters off it’s full name Ta(queria) Ko(rean).

Caught the sunset... found this guy

After that, I went to bed early to get to Grouse Grind the next day!
