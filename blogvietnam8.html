Weird Stuff

We went to a night food market one evening! I ended up eating squid, octopus, and crocodile. I had never tried Octopus before and to be honest, it was a disappointment. However, crocodile was really good!

Woah.

Crocodile

Octopus

On our way back from the Mekong Delta, I shared rat and eel with an American traveller. The rats are tree rats (so not like something found in a sewer...). Not sure how to describe eel, but rat tasted like a weird chicken.

After the War Museum in Ho Chi Minh City, we were waiting out the rain at a restaurant across the street. We tried frog legs – which was good too! Again, not sure how to describe... almost like chicken wings
