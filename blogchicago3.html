On the Water

On Sunday morning, we have breakfast and head to church with my Dad's cousin (who is, crazy enough, born on the same day as my Dad and I, November 6th). We go to church, and the priest is Indian... of course, we are somehow related/connected. Naturally, we head to the liquor store after that... My Dad wanted to get some liquor since its waaaaaaayyy cheaper in the states, especially if you get the 1.75L bottles. We really get ripped off in Canada, it seems like alcohol is cheaper everywhere else in the world.

After that, we head to the Shedd Aquarium since my Dad's cousin recommended it. But... honestly, Marineland is way better. There were some cool animals... but the water shows could not compare. Our last stop is a boat tour downtown Chicago. Although a bit pricey, it was definitely worth it! There is a river that goes through Chicago and the tour guide talks about the architecture of all the buildings, pretty cool stuff. Apparently, one of the Batman movies had some filming done in Chicago!

We call it a night after the tour, and we decide to head out early the next morning to avoid traffic.
