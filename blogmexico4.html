Holbox, Yucatan

Holbox (pronounced Hobosh) is a little island off the north coast of Yucatan (the state in which you will find Cancun). It’s a three-hour bus ride from Tulum so I got there just after lunch after taking a ferry from the mainland.

The island does not have good drainage.

I unpacked and decided to walk long the beach to Playa Coco on the island. This area is supposed to have the best sunsets and it did not disappoint. I got a ton of photos of birds hanging out by the beach. However, the highlight was the flamingos at sunset.

I had to work the following day as I didn’t have a vacation day. It was a pretty productive day even though the internet was poor. I was able to catch up on all the unread messages and things I had missed that day.

To be honest, I was also a bit sick, so I didn’t do much else and ate my way through the next 48 hours before heading to Cancun for my flight back.
