Lantau Island

Getting here was tough... we took the MTR and then a bus to go up (and down at times) the hill to the Big Buddha and Po Lin Monastery. The ride was winding and made us a bit nauseous. However, it was worth it as this was probably my favourite place that we visited in Hong Kong. It was a peaceful escape from the bustling city of Hong Kong and it felt less humid than the main city. The scenery was beautiful and I was able to get some awesome pictures!
